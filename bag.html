I wanted to combine a few things that I like, in order to meet some needs of mine that I couldn't fulfill using other products and systems. The final product looks quite different to my original plans, I lack that visualisation and experience... As I went, certain parts of my original plan just wouldn't work out, I had to improvise. Functionality



I here try to explain some of it's features, please cycle through the pictures below:

Thanks to my grandma and auntie for letting me use their domestic machines, and to my brother for reluctantly agreeing to our 10 minute modelling session.

It's focused on some things I need to carry close to body and securely whilst cycling. The main sleeve is for an attache case and the molle removable pockets are for smaller bits and bobs.

To wear the molle pockets without the main attache case sleeve, we can clip them onto the yoke instead (right).

Here is the bare yoke and straps.

The pockets connect to the yoke via 6 25mm side release buckles. The shoulder straps connect to both the yoke and the main attache case sleeve via 2 25mm slide release buckles around the waist and 2 25mm webbing adjusters on the top of the shoulders (labelled as "bag to strap attachment point" above).
