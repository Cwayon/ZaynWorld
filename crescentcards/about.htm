   About Us

 


Crescent Cards presents a unique collection of exclusively designed hand - crafted and printed cards.

Each design is inspired by the geometry of Islamic design and art forms including architecture, calligraphy, textiles and metalwork.

The giving of a Crescent Card encompasses the unique and distinct elements of Islamic art and its diversity stretching from the Atlantic to China and beyond, embracing over a thousand years of history.

Crescent Cards was originally established in 2005 as a portal to present the creative designs offered by Crescent Cards. Over the years Crescent Cards has evolved into a commercial operation selling cards mainly to the United Kingdom and United States. Crescent cards consists of two partners, Salma and Iffat.
