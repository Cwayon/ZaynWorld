   Shipping Details


Currency Calculator


Crescent Cards offer a tiered shipping rate for all customers (both UK and international).

Shipping rates are calculated automatically at checkout.

If your order falls in between the following values, the corresponding postage and packing rates apply:
 
   £  0.00 - £09.99 = £1.00
   £10.00 - £24.99 = £1.50
   £25.00 - £49.99 = £2.50
   £50.00 - £99.99 = £5.00
   £100+               = £10.00

Remember, the above rates apply to ALL customers, regardless of shipping address. We DO NOT charge higher rates for international customers.

We aim to dispatch all cards within 24 hours of the order being placed.

For larger orders (100+ cards), please allow up to 5 working days for cards to be dispatched.

UK orders are posted via  Royal Mail First Class or Parcel Post.
International orders are sent via Air Mail.
